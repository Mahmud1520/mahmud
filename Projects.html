Design for per day 8000 unit of Solar Cell production

January 2025

This project presents a comprehensive design for a solar cell manufacturing facility with a daily production capacity of 8,000 units. Developed as part of the Materials Processing Plant Design course at Khulna University of Engineering & Technology (KUET), the project focuses on increasing solar cell output while optimizing cost and maintaining high efficiency and product quality. The plant operates on a batch production system, producing 8,400 cells per day and 2.52 million annually. It includes a detailed layout of machinery, production phases—from raw material preparation to final packaging—and considers sustainability, automation, and economic viability. Located in Mirsharai, Chattogram, the facility benefits from port access, a skilled workforce, and excellent transportation links. The financial analysis forecasts a strong return on investment (56.4%), a payback period of 3.1 years, and annual net profits exceeding 26 million BDT. The project supports the global transition to renewable energy and showcases a scalable, eco-friendly, and economically sustainable production model.
